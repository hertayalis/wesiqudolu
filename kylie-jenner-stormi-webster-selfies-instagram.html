    Stormi Webster was born ready for her close-up. (As the daughter of social media queen, Kylie Jenner, and newest member of one of the world's most famous families, it's in her blood.)

    The month-old star showed off her posing skills in her mother's new Instagram photos, shared this afternoon. Webster is cute as a button in the black-and-white selfies, which Jenner snapped from bed. Kylie switches from smiling, to smirking, to blowing a kiss, but nothing can quite compare to Stormi's adorable face.

    Just look at those cheeks:

    Kylie has shared a handful of Stormi photos and videos since giving birth to her on February 1, but these mark the first selfies she's posted of them together. (Hey, every baby milestone counts.) We've previously seen the stylish infant in a sweet mother-daughter photoshoot, which Jenner shared in honor of her daughter's one-month birthday:

    And let's not forget that record-breaking first photo Kylie shared of Stormi holding her finger a few days after she was born:

    Even Stormi's father, Travis Scott, has jumped on the baby photo-sharing train. He first revealed the "little rager"'s face earlier this month.

    For Kylie, being a new mother isn't only about sharing the most Instagrammable moments with her 106 million followers.

    "It’s just a whole new experience. It’s something you never have gone through, but you figure it out as you go," Kylie's best friend, Jordyn Woods, told People of Jenner's transition into motherhoood. "I think that it takes a really strong woman to carry a child."

    Even momager Kris Jenner praised her 20-year-old daughter's parenting skills. "She’s amazing. An amazing mom," the momager told People.

    We're certain Stormi's selfies today won't be her last.
