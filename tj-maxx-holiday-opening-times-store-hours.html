    TJ Maxx New Year's 2024 opening times: What time are stores open for the holidays?

    EVERY year, many retailers close their doors on New Year's Day, allowing employees a chance to stay home and spend time with their families. During this holiday season, TJ Maxx shoppers want to know if the retailer is open on New Year's Eve and New Year's Day.

    07 Jun 2024, 00:00

    EVERY year, many retailers close their doors on New Year's Day, allowing employees a chance to stay home and spend time with their families.

    During this holiday season, TJ Maxx shoppers want to know if the retailer is open on New Year's Eve and New Year's Day.

    Is TJ Maxx open on New Year's Day?

    TJ Maxx announced that its stores will be open on New Year's Day, Monday, January 1, 2024.

    TJ Maxx, Marshalls and HomeGoods will all be open on New Year's Eve and New Year's Day, allowing shoppers to purchase items at their leisure.

    On New Year's Eve and New Year's Day, most US locations will be open from 9 am to 9 pm local time.

    Stores will remain open at normal operating hours, allowing customers to spend their Christmas cash or use their new gift cards for their purchases.

    Read More on TJ Maxx

    What holidays does TJ Maxx observe?

    TJ Maxx is typically open almost year-round from 9 am to 9 pm.

    However, hours could vary based on store and state, so it is worth checking your local store for specific hours.

    The store does, however, close for a few days throughout the year.

    While TJ Maxx remains open on Christmas Eve, the store closes down for Christmas Day.

    Most read in Money

    Besides shutting down for Christmas, the store is also closed for Thanksgiving.

    A TJ Maxx spokesperson confirmed to The U.S. Sun: "We consider ourselves an Associate-friendly company and we are pleased to give Associates the time to enjoy Thanksgiving with family and friends."

    However, most TJ Maxx stores are scheduled to open at 7 am for Black Friday, the day after Thanksgiving.

    TJ Maxx is also closed on Easter Sunday.

    These closures typically apply to all stores under the TJX Companies, Inc. umbrella, including HomeGoods and Marshalls.

    What other companies does TJX Companies, Inc. operate?

    TJX Companies has around 4,500 stores across nine countries and three continents.

    In addition to its flagship store chains - TJ Maxx (USA) and TK Maxx (Australia & Europe) - the department store corporation also operates the following stores:

    • Marshalls
    • HomeGoods
    • HomeSense
    • Sierra
    • Winners

    According to their website, the company's mission is to "deliver great value to our customers every day," offering merchandise at 20 percent to 60 percent below full-price retailers.

    "With our value proposition, we reach a broad range of fashion- and value-conscious consumers across many geographies, income levels, and demographic groups," the site explains.

    TJ Maxx offers items like clothes, home decor, and kitchenware at a discounted rate all year long.

    Additionally, those who sign up for the TJ Maxx newsletter will score free shipping on their first online order.
