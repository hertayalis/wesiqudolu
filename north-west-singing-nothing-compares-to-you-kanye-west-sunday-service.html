    It's pretty much a given that North West has inherited her dad's musical talent. Attending Kanye West's Sunday Service, North has taken part on several occasions, and this weekend she performed in a super sweet rendition of Sinéad O'Connor's "Nothing Compares 2U."

    As part of a children's choir at the Sunday Service, North performed alongside her little brother Saint, and cousin Penelope Disick. Footage of the performance was shared by Big Sean on his Instagram Stories, who was joined in the congregation by Kim Kardashian West, Kourtney and Khloé Kardashian, according to People.

    Clearly, North West is a little star in the making, and the way that she sweetly chaperones Saint is beyond adorable:

    A set list revealed the other tracks that were performed at the Sunday Service, but North and Saint's rendition of "Nothing Compares 2U" was definitely the highlight.

    During a previous Sunday Service, North took the mic and full-on performed for the entire congregation.
